Vishwakarma Institute of Technology

The institute was established in 1983 and it is affiliated to the Savitribai Phule Pune University.

Vishwakarma Institute of Technology (VIT) is an autonomous engineering college located in Upper Indira Nagar, Bibwewadi, Pune, Maharashtra, India. The institute was established in 1983 and it is affiliated to the Savitribai Phule Pune University. The college is largely supported by the Bansilal Ramanath Agarwal Charitable Trust. VIT was the first private college in Maharashtra to attain academic autonomy in the academic year 2008-2009. The college is the only college in Pune to provide Honors and Minors courses in addition to normal undergraduate courses.

It offers various courses in UG, PG and PhD courses like B.Tech, M.tech, MCA and PhD programmes that are provided with professional expertise. There a various department in the college like Chemical engineering, industrial engineering, Information Engineering, Information technology and mca, Electronics and telecommunications engineering, Industrial and production engineering, engineering, science and humanities, instrumentation engineering. Various events happen at VIT, Pune like the Technical campus, Technical forums, Cultural events, annual events, Students club. The college has established Bajrangdas Lohiya Central library that consists of reference section, journal section, reading hall and stack - room. The educational session for Semester is carried out for minimum 90 working days for a Semester as per University Grants Commission (UGC), All India Council for Technical Education (AICTE) and University of Pune norms. Advance planning is carried for the upcoming academic year and the follows the calendar year as per the norms and policies. Following activities are covered during the year which are: 1. College re-opening after vacation Students' registration and Director's address Teaching Learning activities – inclusive of Remedial teaching 2. Submission deadlines Examination and Assessment Schedule 3. Student Activity Details Schedule of various meetings / Audits etc. 4. Vacations and holidays
